FAQ

  • Do I need an account? No. RedTag is single-user and does not require login.
  • Does RedTag use a server? No. Data is stored locally on your device.
  • How do I send notices? RedTag uses the iOS Share Sheet to send via Text (SMS) or Email.
  • How do I calculate backcharges? Set Fee/Day by Delay Type, then mark items complete with a completion date.
  • How do I export reports? Export CSV/PDF from the Dashboard/Reports screens.
  • How do I back up data? Use Backup/Restore to export/import a JSON file.

Privacy Policy

RedTag does not require an account and does not transmit your data to a server.

Data you enter (such as addresses, trade contact details, notes, dates, and optional photos) is stored locally on your device.

You control sharing when you send notices using your device’s messaging and email apps.
